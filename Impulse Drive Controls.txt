WASD - Move
Mouse - Look around
Right Click - place/shoot shock wave emitter
Left Click - to delete placed shock wave emitters
E - Change Shock wave emitter
Emitter 1 - Launches player in a specified direction
Emitter 2 - Fires an emitter that attaches to an object
Q - Causes emitter to explode and generate a shock wave
F/G - Cycles through Emitter 1's launch direction
